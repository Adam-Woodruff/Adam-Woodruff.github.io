About me

Hello,

My name is Adam, and I am an A level student studying maths, computer science and 3D design. I consider myself a tenacious and motivated person with a broad skill set.

I currently work as a recreational assistant at my local leisure centre. This job comes with a lot of responsibility as not only am I responsible for helping open and close the centre but also during the day as a lifeguard where I ensure the public is safe.

In my spare time I also enjoy volunteering at my local scout group where every week I help organise activities, on top of this I am a keen athlete taking part in local running events and swimming regularly to ensure I am the best I can be at my job.
